= Scheduler

include::{includedir}/_header.adoc[]

**plentymarkets » Orders » Scheduler**

The __**scheduler**__ is a __**subscription module**__. This module allows your customers to subscribe to certain items in your online store. The scheduler menu is only visible in your plentymarkets system if you **__booked__** this module.

[TIP]
.Tip
====
Go to **plentymarkets » Start » plentymarkets account » Service Center » Tab: Contract** to __**book**__ the module.
====
