==  Info: revenue by payment method

This statistic shows the **revenue** that was generated by a particular **payment method** during a selected **time span**. Data is displayed for those sales orders that fall into a selected **sales order status** range. As an alternative to selecting a certain predefined time span, you can also choose an individual **start** and **end date**. Default setting for the **time span**: the **last 30 days**. Default setting of the **status section**: From **status 1** to **status 7**.

[TIP]
.Tip
====
This statistic is available **starting with plentymarkets version 4.501**.
====

==  Details about the statistic

The name of the **payment method** and the corresponding **revenue in Euro** will be displayed for the selected **time span**. You will also see the **number of sales orders** that were generated for each payment method. If a payment method did not generate any revenue, then this payment method will not be displayed. The name of the **payment method**, the corresponding **revenue in Euro** and the **number of sales orders** that generated this revenue will be displayed as **information in a mouseover** (see image 1). In this statistic you can see, e.g. which payment methods generated the most revenue and are therefore particularly lucrative for your company.

**Show details (mouseover)**

Point your **cursor** at one of the elements in the statistic in order to see **details** about that particular element.

[TIP]
.Tip
====
In image 1, you can see that the payment method **ClickAndBuy ONE** corresponds to a total revenue of **2,357.83 Euros** and that this revenue was generated by **8 sales orders**.
