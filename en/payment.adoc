One important aspect of your online store is the selection of payment methods offered. If the right selection is missing, customers could cancel the purchase at the end of the order process or not order in your online store at all. To avoid this, you should find out which payment methods your customers prefer to use. This area of the manual describes how to set up payment methods and payment providers in plentymarkets.

[IMPORTANT]
.Callisto or Ceres online store?
====
The process of integrating different payment methods and payment providers depends on whether you run your online store based on Callisto or Ceres. The payment providers for <<#300, Callisto stores>> are integrated via interfaces. If you use Ceres or a Callisto store in combination with a Ceres checkout, you have to download the respective <<#400, plugins>> in the plentyMarketplace.
