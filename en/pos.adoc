== What is plentymarkets POS?

plentymarkets POS (*Point of Sale*) is our in-house developed solution for managing till sales at your brick and mortar store. The plentymarkets POS functionalities are integrated as a component that is part of 
the link:https://knowledge.plentymarkets.com/en/app[plentymarkets app^].
This manual page offers you an overview on where to find information on setting up plentymarkets POS and using its functionalities to their best.

[TIP]
.POS demo mode
====
Are you interested in using plentymarkets POS and want to take a look at its functions without any obligation? Check out our detailed instructions on using the plentymarkets POS demo mode <<pos/demo#, here>>.
====
