== Content of this chapter

This chapter deals with the processes in plentymarkets that can be configured with the plentymarkets client. +
The plentymarkets Client is available starting with plentymarkets version 5.000.

For further information and a link to the download, refer to the **link:https://www.plentymarkets.co.uk/product/processes/plentymarkets-client/[plentymarkets Client]** page on our website.
