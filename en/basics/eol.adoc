This overview does not replace the link:https://forum.plentymarkets.com/c/changelog[Changelog^] thread or the thread sticked to the corresponding category in the link:https://forum.plentymarkets.com/[plentymarkets forum^]. Rather, this page provides a central overview to ensure that this information does not get lost due to the large amount of threads in the plentymarkets forum.
