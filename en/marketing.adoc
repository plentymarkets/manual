== Content of this chapter

In this chapter, we describe various possibilities for increasing your online store's efficiency through **marketing activities**. All of the possibilities presented here can be accomplished with plentymarkets.

[TIP]
.Tip: **You will learn ...**
====
... how you can configure **cross-selling items** in your online store and automatically offer them to your customers.
====

... how you can use **newsletters**, **coupons** and **sweepstakes** to increase communication with your customers and generate sales in the process.

... the benefits of having a **Trustbadge** like the one given by **Trusted Shops**.

... and much more.
