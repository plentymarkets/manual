:key-figure: Marketplace stock movements

The key figure *{key-figure}* allows you to visualise stock movements to markets on your dashboard. As such, you can check at what time what stock was communicated to a market for a variation.

You currently can visualise the marketplace stock movements to the following markets using the key figure *{key-figure}*:

* Amazon

[IMPORTANT]
.The key figure *{key-figure}* is new!
====
The key figure *{key-figure}* is a new feature. We are working on visualising the stock movements to additional markets.
