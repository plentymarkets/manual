==  General information about Twenga

With over 300 million products listed by more than 200.000 merchants and millions of users, link:http://www.twenga.co.uk/[**Twenga**^] is one of the most comprehensive shopping search engines available. Twenga uses a unique technology to list all shop and product information available online on just one site. The company does business in 15 countries, e.g. in France, Germany, Australia and Spain. Twenga gives you the possibility to present your items and to grow your customer base.

==  Registering with Twenga

In order to take advantage of the services provided by Twenga, you need to register first. In doing so, you will receive the **Twenga hashkey** that you need for the configuration in plentymarkets. Contact link:https://rts.twenga.co.uk/signup[**Twenga**^] to register.

==  Activating Twenga in plentymarkets

In order to activate Twenga for your standard store, save your unique **Twenga hashkey** that you received when registering with Twenga.

image::omni-channel/online-store/extras/assets/EN-Settings-Clients-Standard-Ext-Twenga-01-SI.png[]

__Image 1: **activating** Twenga__

[.instruction]
Activating Twenga:

.  Go to **Settings » Client (store) » Standard » Services » Twenga**.
.  Select the **Ready to sell** service from the drop-down menu.
.  Enter the **hashkey** that you received from Twenga.
.  Place a **check mark** next to the option **Active** to activate the module.
.  Click on the **save icon** to save the settings.

As soon as you have set up Twenga in plentymarkets your items will be retrieved and integrated.

[IMPORTANT]
.Clients
====
Twenga needs to be individually activated for each __**client**__ in plentymarkets. This means that you need to separately register with Twenga for each client. You will receive a new **hashkey** for each client.
====
