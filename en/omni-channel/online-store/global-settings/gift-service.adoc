== General information about the gift service

Set up a gift service in your online store, e.g. if you want to allow your customers to have their items gift wrapped. This page contains information about setting up the gift service in plentymarkets.

== Creating items

Create items for each of the services that you would like to offer in the order process. For example, create the items **Wrapping paper** and **Gift wrap**.

image::omni-channel/online-store/global-settings/assets/EN-Settings-Client-Global-Gift-Service-01.png[]

__Image 1: gift service items__

[.instruction]
Creating items for the gift service:

. Go to **Item » Edit item**.
. Click on the **New** button to create a new item.
. Enter a **name**, e.g. cellophane wrap.
. Enter a **price**.
. Click on the **save icon** to save the settings.
. If needed, create additional items for the rest of your gift service offers.

[TIP]
.Offering different colors
====
If you would like to offer wrapping paper in various **colors**, then create **variations** that correspond to the gift service items. For further information, refer to the **<<omni-channel/online-store/global-settings/external-services/faceted-search#390, Attributes >>** page of the manual.
====

[TIP]
.Tip
====
Enter a large enough amount of **stock** to ensure that the service will always be available.
====

== Linking items and activating the function

In the next step, link the gift service to all of the gift items that you want to offer your customers in the order process. Then activate the function. To do so, proceed as follows.

image::omni-channel/online-store/global-settings/assets/EN-Settings-Client-Global-Gift-Service-02.png[]

__Image 2: linking gift service items__

[.instruction]
Linking items and activating the gift service:

. Go to **Settings » Client (store) » Global settings » Gift service**.
. Enter the **name** and the **item ID** of the gift service item.
. Set the **Active** option to **Yes**.
. Click on the **save icon** to save the settings. +
→ The gift service is now activated in the order process. Customers will be offered the gift items that were entered.

== Appearance in the shopping cart

The linked items will be listed in the order process after clicking on the **Select gift service** link (image 3, red arrows). The customer can then click on the link and select one of the gift wraps that are offered.

image::omni-channel/online-store/global-settings/assets/EN-Settings-Client-Global-Gift-Service-03.png[]

__Image 3: gift service; display in the shopping cart__

[TIP]
.Appearance in the order overview and on the invoice
====
When looking at the order overview or the invoice, the stock unit for the gift wrap is displayed directly below the stock unit for the item that is gift wrapped.
====

== FAQs

**Q1**: The **price** of a gift service item is displayed as **0.00** in the sales order process even though a price has been saved for it.

**A1**: Go to **Settings » Client (store) » Global settings » Gift service** and make sure that an item ID was saved for this item. This error can be due to the wrong item ID.
