==  General information

__**Cliplister**__ offers shop owners a variety of __**product-related video clips**__. Along with __**item descriptions**__ and __**pictures**__, these provide an additional impression of the product. This offers several advantages for your online store: Increased attention from visitors, optimized product presentation, precise product information, a lasting increase in conversion and a strengthening of your market presence.

This page contains information about how to activate Cliplister in your online store.

==  Activation

Before you can __**activate**__ Cliplister for your online store, you have to __**register**__ on the **link:http://www.cliplister.com/[Cliplister website, window="_blank"]**. You will receive access data that will have to be saved in plentymarkets.

[.instruction]
Saving the access data

.  Go to **Cliplister** (see path above).
.  Enter the data that you received upon registration with Cliplister into the fields **Customer number** and **Crypt key**.
.  Click on the **save icon** to save the settings.

image::omni-channel/online-store/global-settings/external-services/assets/EN-ENG-Mandant-GlobEinstell-ExtDienste-Cliplister-01-SI.png[]

__Image 1: Cliplister **access data**__

The **Customer number** and **Crypt key** that you enter here will be valid for all of your __**clients (stores)**__. It is not currently possible to use individual Cliplister accounts for different clients (stores). However, you can save different language and display settings for Cliplister videos for your individual clients (stores).

Once the activation has been completed, Cliplister can be configured in the menu **<<omni-channel/online-store/standard/external-services/cliplister#, Settings » Client (store) » Standard » Services » Cliplister  >>**.
