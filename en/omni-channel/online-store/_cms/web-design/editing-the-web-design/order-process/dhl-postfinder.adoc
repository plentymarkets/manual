== General information about DHL Postfinder

*DHL Postfinder* offers a convenient search function. This feature shows your customer a list of *post offices* and *Packstations* nearby. The list is displayed when your customer enters his or her zip code in the My account area or during the <<omni-channel/online-store/cms#web-design-editing-the-web-design-order-process-checkout, checkout>> process. Customers can then use the desired post office or Packstation as their *delivery address*.

=== CSS My account

For display in the *My account* area, the *z index* of the *CSS* needs to be customised.

.customising the *z index*
image::omni-channel/online-store/_cms/web-design/editing-the-web-design/order-process/assets/EN-CMS-Web-Design-Editing-Order-Process-DHL-Postfinder-01-SI.png[]

[.instruction]
Configuring the CSS for My account:

. Go to *CMS » Web design » Layout » CSS » CSSMyAccount*.
. Search for the line (search function of your browser): "z-index: *400010*;". +
*Important*: Select the editor setting *Text field* in the upper right corner of the *toolbar*.
. Change the line to "z-index: *40010*;".
. *Save* the CSS.

=== Texts

Customise the *texts* to display in the *online store* and the *my account* area.

.customising *texts*, e.g. *My Account » Submenu: Change delivery address*
image::omni-channel/online-store/_cms/web-design/editing-the-web-design/order-process/assets/EN-CMS-Web-Design-Editing-Order-Process-DHL-Postfinder-02-SI.png[]

[.instruction]
Configuring texts:

. Go to *CMS » Web design » Order process » Checkout » Submenu: Delivery address*.
. Customise the following *texts*: +
*PostNummer*: Enter *PostNummer*. +
*Search Packstation / post office*: Enter *Search Packstation or post office*.
. *Save* the settings.
. Go to *CMS » Web design » My account » Submenu: Change delivery address*.
. Customise the following *texts*: +
*Additional address information*: Enter *Additional address information*. +
*PostNummer*: Enter *PostNummer*. +
*Search Packstation / post office*: Enter *Search Packstation or post office*.
. *Save* the settings.

=== Error messages

Customise the *error messages* in the *language packages*.

.customising error messages
image::omni-channel/online-store/_cms/web-design/editing-the-web-design/order-process/assets/EN-CMS-Web-Design-Editing-Order-Process-DHL-Postfinder-03-SI.png[]

[.instruction]
Configuring error messages:

. Go to *System » Client » Settings » Languages*.
. Select the *language* (default: de) and the section *Error messages*.
. Click on the *magnifying glass*. +
→ The error messages are displayed.
. Change the error messages *no. 89* and *no. 93* by changing *PostIdent number* to: *PostNummer*.
. *Save* the settings.

=== Menu: DHL Packstation

You now have to activate the function in the *menu*.

.menu *DHL Packstation*
image::omni-channel/online-store/_cms/web-design/editing-the-web-design/order-process/assets/EN-CMS-Web-Design-Editing-Order-Process-DHL-Postfinder-04-SI.png[]

[.instruction]
Activating DHL Packstation:

. Go to *System » Client » Select client » Order process » DHL Packstation*.
. Select *Yes* for the option *Permit Packstation as the delivery address*.
. *Save* the settings.

== Function

If a customer wants to chose a Packstation or post office as the *delivery address*, he or she can now click on *Send to a different address* during the *Invoice information* step of the *order process*.

An empty address field with the *Postfinder* function is displayed:

.DHL Postfinder during checkout
image::omni-channel/online-store/_cms/web-design/editing-the-web-design/order-process/assets/EN-CMS-Web-Design-Editing-Order-Process-DHL-Postfinder-05-SI.png[]

Here, enter the desired *ZIP code* or *city*. +
It is possible to enter the first three or four digits of the zip code or a few letters of the city's name only. If there are too many Packstations and/or post offices, a DHL error message is displayed and a more precise search entry is necessary (e.g. the full zip code).

[[image-post-offices-packstations-found]]
.display of the post offices and Packstations found
image::omni-channel/online-store/_cms/web-design/editing-the-web-design/order-process/assets/EN-CMS-Web-Design-Editing-Order-Process-DHL-Postfinder-06-SI.png[]

When the customer enters a *zip code* and clicks on *Search Packstation or post office*, the *post offices* and *Packstations* close to this zip code and information about the distance is displayed. The customer selects an option and clicks on *Save*. The post office or station selected is inserted as delivery address.
