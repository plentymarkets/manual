All of the ratings submitted in your online store are also listed in the menu **CMS » Customer feedback**. All different types of feedback are displayed in the overview by default. Use the search options to narrow down the results. For example, if you only want to display a specific type of feedback, feedback that was received during a specific time period or feedback that was submitted by a specific customer.

== Setting up customer feedback

First, set up the module in the menu <<omni-channel/online-store/extras/customer-feedback#, **System » Client » Settings » Feedback**>> and activate the desired types of feedback.

== Editing customer feedback

When evaluations and comments are submitted in your online store, they are also listed in the menu **CMS » Customer feedback**. Here you can make feedback visible or invisible and you can edit comments if they do not meet your standards for netiquette.

[.instruction]
Editing feedback:

. Go to **CMS » Customer feedback**.
. Click on an evaluation to edit it.
. Make the desired changes.
. Click on the **save icon** to save the settings.

[NOTE]
.Changing the visibility
====
Click on the **eye** symbol in the overview to make a feedback visible or invisible in the online store. If a feedback is invisible, then the eye is crossed out by a **red X**. It is possible to open an individual feedback and then change the visibility with the **Visibility** drop-down menu.
====

== Example configuration in the CMS

Templates and template variables are used to display the feature in your online store. Go to **CMS » Web design » Folder: Layout » Folder: Form** to find the appropriate templates and template variables.

=== Setting up Google reCAPTCHA

Set up Google reCAPTCHA for a form for evaluating items in your online store. First, register your online store to use link:https://www.google.com/recaptcha/admin#list[*Google reCAPTCHA*^]. Then, enter the website key and the secret key for your online store.

[.instruction]
Setting up Google reCAPTCHA:

. Go to *System » Client » Select client » Online store » Basic settings*.
. Enter the *Google reCAPTCHA Website key*.
. Enter the *Google reCAPTCHA Secret key*.
. **Save** the settings.
