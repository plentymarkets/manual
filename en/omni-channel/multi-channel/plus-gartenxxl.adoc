link:https://www.plus.de/[**Plus.de**^] offers an extensive portfolio of gardening and DIY supplies, living room and bedroom furniture, sports and wellness products, household items, wine as well as multimedia and entertainment electronics. Since April 2014, the e-commerce activities of Plus Online GmbH are consolidated under the new corporate name Tengelmann E-Stores GmbH.

link:http://www.gartenxxl.de/[**GartenXXL**^] is a brand belonging to the Tengelmann E-Stores GmbH. The GartenXXL website offers brand-name equipment and outdoor furniture as well as tips and tricks for garden maintenance.

You have to set up Plus.de in plentymarkets before you can sell items on the market. To do so, proceed as follows:

== Setting the item availability

Items have to be available for Plus.de. This is done in the **Availability** tab of an item within the **<<item/managing-items#40, Item » Edit item » Tab: Variation ID>>** menu.

[.instruction]
Setting the item availability for Plus.de:

. Open the **Item » Edit item » Open item » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Settings** menu.
. Activate the main variation in the **Availability** section.
. Click on the **Availability** tab.
. Click in the **Markets** section in the selection field.  +
→ A list with all available markets will be displayed.
. Activate the option **Plus.de**.
. Activate the option **GartenXXL** if you also want to sell your items there.
. Activate the option **Web API**.
. Click on **Add**. +
→ The Market will be added.
. **Save** the settings.  +
→ The item is available on Plus.de.

The availability for variations can be individually edit in the **Item » Edit item » Open item » Tab: Variation ID » Open variation » Tab: Availability** menu.

== Linking categories

You need to create category links in order for your items to be displayed on the markets Plus.de and GartenXXL.de. Plus.de will send you the necessary Plus.de and GartenXXL.de category IDs. Proceed as follows to create category links in order for your items to be displayed in the <<item/categories#, **categories**>> on Plus.de and GartenXXL.de.

[.instruction]
Linking categories:

. Go to **Settings » Markets » Plus.de**.
. Click on the **Activate categories** tab.
. In the columns **Plus.de category links** and **GartenXXL.de category links**, enter the category ID that best matches your online store categories. +
→ The Plus.de and GartenXXL.de category IDs can be found in the category files that you received from Plus.de.
. Activate the categories.
. **Save** the settings.

[#60]
== Linking manufacturers

You need to link your **<<item/managing-items#560, manufacturers>>**  in order for your items to be displayed for sale on the markets Plus.de and GartenXXL.de. You will receive a file from Plus.de which contains the manufacturer IDs.

[.instruction]
Linking manufacturers:

. Go to **System » Item » Manufacturers**.
. Open the manufacturer.
. Enter the ID into the **Plus.de ID** field. +
→ You can find the Plus.de manufacturer ID in the file that you received from Plus.de.
. **Save** the settings.

== Creating properties

Use **<<item/managing-items#480, properties>>**  to link your items to particular characteristics that you need for the market Plus.de. If needed, you can save commissions and category links that differ from the default settings saved for the items. Moreover, you transfer item information to Plus.de with the help of property filters and determine criteria for the item search on Plus.de.

As described in chapter **<<omni-channel/multi-channel/plus-gartenxxl#50, Linking categories>>**, go to **Settings » Markets » Plus.de** and save the standard category links in the **Activate categories** tab. If you want to link specific items to a different category, use the **<<item/managing-items#480, property>>** **Item category link for GartenXXL.de** or **Item category link for Plus.de**.

[.instruction]
Creating a property:

. Go to **System » Item » Properties**.
. Click on the **New property** tab.
. Enter a name in the **Back end name** field.
. Select the property type **Text**.
. Select the Plus.de property **Item category link for GartenXXL.de** or **Item category link for Plus.de** from the drop-down menu.
. **Save** the settings.

[#80]
=== Setting commission rates saved for an item

Go to **Settings » Markets » Plus.de** to save a standard commission rate for the market Plus.de. You can save a different commission rate for each item because different commissions may apply to particular Plus.de item classes. To do so, use the **<<item/managing-items#480, property>>** **Commission in percent**. This setting has a higher priority than the standard provision. Make sure to coordinate this percentage value with Plus.de in advance.

[.instruction]
Creating a property:

. Go to **System » Item » Properties**.
. Click on the **New property** tab.
. Enter a name in the **Back end name** field.
. Select the property type **Text**.
. Select the Plus.de property **Commission in percent** from the drop-down menu.
. **Save** the settings.

[TIP]
.Properties: filter
====
Filters for Plus.de have two functions: First, they are used to transfer item information to Plus.de and second, items can be found with filters on Plus.de. After having selected a filter, another drop-down menu will be displayed. Select the filter property that you want to link to the item. +
Note: The property **Energy efficiency** is only available for the market Plus.de.
====

=== Creating the property energy efficiency

To comply with EU regulations on energy efficiency that came into force on 1 January 2015, you have to provide information on the energy efficiency of certain items to sell them on Plus.de. In addition, you have to make product data sheets available to potential buyers of these items. Create the following three **<<item/managing-items#480, properties>>** : **Energy efficiency class**, **Energy label** and **Product data sheet**.

[.instruction]
Creating the property energy efficiency class:

. Go to **System » Item » Properties**.
. Click on the **New property** tab.
. Enter a name in the **Back end name** field.
. Select the property type **No**.
. Select the option **Energy efficiency** from the **Plus.de property** drop-down menu. +
→ Another drop-down menu will open.
. Select the energy efficiency class.
. **Save** the settings.

Select the property type **File** for the properties Energy label and Product data sheet. Select the option **Energy label** or **Product data sheet** from the drop-down menu **Plus.de property**. The following example describes how to create the property energy label. Afterwards, create the property product data sheet the same way.

[.instruction]
Creating the property energy label:

. Go to **System » Item » Properties**.
. Click on the **New property** tab.
. Enter a name in the **Back end name** field.
. Select the property type **File**.
. Select the option **Energy label** from the drop-down menu **Plus.de property**.
. **Save** the settings.

You then link the properties in the **<<item/managing-items#480, Properties>>**  tab of a Plus.de item. Proceed as described below to do so.

[.instruction]
Linking energy efficiency properties to a Plus.de item:

. Go to **Item » Edit item**.
. Open the item.
. Click on the **Properties** tab.
. Activate the properties **Energy efficiency class**, **Energy label** and **Product data sheet** that you have just created.
. **Save** the settings. +
→ The properties are linked with the item and are visible in the **Properties** area.
. Upload the image file of the energy label to the **Energy label** property.
. Upload the PDF file of the product data sheet to the **Product data sheet** property.
. **Save** the settings.

Instead of linking the **Energy label** property, you can also upload the energy label file to the **<<item/managing-items#660, Images>>**  tab of the item. To do so, proceed as follows.

[.instruction]
Saving the energy label in the "Images" tab:

. Go to **Item » Edit item**.
. Open the item.
. Click on the **Images** tab.
. Add the image file of the energy label either via **Select files** or via the drag &amp; drop function.
. **Save** the settings.
. Go to **Settings » Markets » Plus.de**.
. In the **Settings** tab, enter the image's position number into **Image position for energy label** field (table 1).
. **Save** the settings.

== Creating delivery notes and return labels

The following information only applies to delivery notes and return labels for Plus.de and GartenXXL.de. It does not apply to other markets.

[IMPORTANT]
.Invoices are sent by Plus.de
====
Plus.de sends the invoice to the customer. You will receive the customer's delivery address from Plus.de but not the customer's invoice address. The delivery address supplied by Plus.de is saved as the delivery and invoice address in plentymarkets. You send the items to this address.
====

Proceed as follows to create **<<order-processing/orders/order-documents#170, PDF templates>>** for delivery notes and return labels for Plus.de and GartenXXL.de. The following example describes how to create a delivery note for Plus.de. Afterwards, create a return label for Plus.de in the same way. Proceed the same way to create these two documents also for GartenXXL.de. The logo of Plus.de or GartenXXL.de must be included in the PDF templates. Request the logos directly from Plus.de.

[.instruction]
Creating delivery note for Plus.de (German example):

. Go to **Settings » Client (store) » Select store » Locations » Germany (standard) » Documents » Delivery note**.
. Click on the **PDF template » New template** tab.
. Select the **Plus.de** option from the **Payment method** drop-down menu.
. In the **File** line, click on **Browse...**. +
→ Upload the PDF template.
. **Save** the settings.

[IMPORTANT]
.Mandatory document entries for Plus.de and GartenXXL.de
====
The external order number and the external customer number are mandatory on documents that belong to orders with the referrer Plus.de or GartenXXL.de. You can normally hide these entries by selecting **NO** in the **Template** tab's **Optional elements in the stock unit table** area of the **Settings » Client (store) » Select store » Locations » Germany (standard) » Documents » Delivery note** and/or **Return label** menu. +
For Plus.de and GartenXXL.de, the external order number and the external customer number are not hidden, even if you select the option **NO** here.
====

[IMPORTANT]
.Note will be hidden
====
The text that you can save in the **Note** field in the **Template** tab's **Optional elements in the stock unit table** area of the **Settings » Client (store) » Select store » Locations » Germany (standard) » Documents » Delivery note** and/or **Return label** menu is hidden on documents that belong to the referrers Plus.de and GartenXXL.de. This is required by Plus.de.
====
