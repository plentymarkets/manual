= Category discounts
:lang: en
include::{includedir}/_header.adoc[]
:keywords: Store, Client, Categories, Discounts
:position: 45

Go to **Settings » Client (store) » Standard » Categories » Activate** to save volume discounts for individual item categories. You can save up to three volume discounts per category. The discounts are applied automatically when customers place at least the specified number of items of this category. The specified discounts will only be calculated for an item's default category.

[.instruction]
Saving category discounts:

. Go to **Settings » Client (store) » Standard » Categories » Discounts**.
. Carry out the settings. Pay attention to the explanations given in table 1.
. **Save** the settings. +
→ The discount is applied automatically when customers add the required number of items to the online store's shopping cart.

.Saving category discounts
[cols="a,a"]
|====
|Setting |Explanation

|**Discount 1-3 (%)**
|Enter the discount in percent.

|**Minimum quantity**
|Enter the number of items customers need to order from this category for the discount to take effect.
|====
