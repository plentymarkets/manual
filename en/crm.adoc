In this area, you will find information about creating and editing <<crm/managing-contacts#100, contact data records>> - from the address via orders to tickets, documents and much more. Create <<crm/managing-contacts#15, customer classes>>, <<crm/managing-contacts#20, types>> and use the <<crm/managing-contacts#25, discount types>> in plentymarkets.

Furthermore, learn how to use the <<crm/using-the-ticket-system#, ticket system>> for your customer communication and how to retain control over orders that are still unpaid with the help of the <<crm/managing-contacts#5200, AR list>>.

All emails that are sent with plentymarkets are based on email templates. You can define up to 2 attachments for each email template. Learn in this area how to <<crm/sending-emails#1200, create email templates>>.

Set up a <<crm/sending-newsletters#, newsletter>> service to automatically inform your customers at regular intervals about news in your online store.
