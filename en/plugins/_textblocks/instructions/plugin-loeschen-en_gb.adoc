Deleting plugins:

. Go to *Plugins » Plugin set overview*.
. Click on more actions (icon:ellipsis-v[]) in the row of the plugin you want to delete.
. Click on *Delete plugin* (icon:trash-o[role=”red”]). +
→ The plugin is removed from the plugin set.
