[#100]
== Registering with PIXmania

First register with link:https://www.pixmania-pro.co.uk/secure/gb/uk/s_action/register/index.html[PIXmania^] in order to set up the interface to PIXmania in your plentymarkets system. You will then receive the access data that you need for the setup in plentymarkets.

[#200]
== Setting up PIXmania in plentymarkets

Requirements for using PIXmania in your plentymarkets system:

* Items for PIXmania need to have the manufacturer and at least one item image saved for them.
* You need your PIXmania seller key, which you received from PIXmania after registering for this market.

Country platforms become available for selection after you have saved your seller key.

[.instruction]
Setting up PIXmania in plentymarkets:

. Go to *Setup » Markets » PIXmania » Settings*.
. Expand the *Basic settings* section (icon:plus-square-o[]).
. Carry out the settings. Pay attention to the explanations given in <<basic-settings-pixmania>>.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

* Daily item export

* Price synchronisation every 15 minutes

== Activating the order referrer

In order to link items, properties etc. with PIXmania, you have to activate the order referrer PIXmania in the *Setup » Orders » Order referrer* menu.

[.instruction]
Activating the order referrer PIXmania:

. Go to *Setup » Orders » Order referrer*.
. Place a check mark next to *PIXmania*.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

== Setting the item availability

Items have to be available for PIXmania. This is done in the *Availability* tab of an item within the *Item » Edit item » Open item » Tab: Variation ID* menu. Items for PIXmania also need to have the manufacturer and at least one item image saved for them.

[.instruction]
Setting the item availability for PIXmania:

. Go to *Item » Edit item » Open item » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Settings*.
. Activate the main variation in the *Availability* section.
. Click on the *Availability* tab.
. Click in the selection field in the *Markets* section.  +
→ A list with all available markets is displayed.
. Place a check mark next to the option *PIXmania*.
. Place a check mark next to the option *Web API*.
. Click on *Add* (icon:plus-square[role="green"]). +
→ The market is added.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings. +
→ The item is available on PIXmania.

The availability for variations can be edited in the *Item » Edit item » Open item » Tab: Variations » Open variation » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Availability* menu.

== Importing brand IDs

The brand IDs from PIXmania are required. You receive them by following the procedure described below.

[.instruction]
Exporting the data format:

. Go to *Data » Dynamic export » Tab: New data format*.
. Select the setting *Producer* under *Data format type*.
. Enter a name.
. Activate the fields *ProducerID*, *ProducerName*, and *ProducerPixmaniaID*.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings. +
→ The *Edit data format* tab opens.
. Expand the data format (icon:plus-square-o[]).
. Click on *Export data*.
. Save the CSV file on your computer.

Send the CSV file to the PIXmania customer service. You will then receive an Excel file from PIXmania. Generate a CSV file from this Excel file which corresponds to the *Producer* data format.

[.instruction]
Importing the data format:

. Go to *Data » Dynamic import*.
. Select the field separator.
. Select your data format under *Format*.
. Click on *Select file*.
. Open the CSV file.
. Click on *Import*. +
→ After having imported the brand IDs, you can find the PIXmania ID of a manufacturer in the *Setup » Item » Manufacturers* menu.

== Linking categories

In order to link your categories with PIXmania categories, download the PIXmania category list first. Then link your own categories with the most appropriate PIXmania categories.

[.instruction]
Importing PIXmania categories:

. Go to *Setup » Markets » PIXmania » Category link*.
. Click on *Select file*.
. Select the *field separator*.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings. +
→ The category list is imported.

Proceed as described below to link your categories with PIXmania categories.

[.instruction]
Linking categories:

. Go to *Setup » Markets » PIXmania » Category link*.
. In the *PIXmania link* section, click on the *gear-wheel icon*. +
→ The PIXmania categories are displayed under *Categories*.
. Expand the category.
. Select the subcategory. +
→ The linked category is displayed in the *Selection* section.
. Click on *Reload*. +
→ The links are displayed under *PIXmania link*.

[IMPORTANT]
.*Deleting category links*
====
You can delete category links in the *Selection* area.
====

After you have linked your categories with PIXmania's categories, export the data to PIXmania.

[.instruction]
Exporting category links:

. Go to *Setup » Markets » PIXmania » Category link*.
. Click on the *Export link* button. +
→ A CSV file is created.
. Save the CSV file on your computer.
. Send the CSV file to PIXmania.

== Linking attributes

If you have created <<item/managing-items#390, attributes>> in your plentymarkets system, you have to link these attributes with PIXmania. Proceed as described below to link the attribute Colour with PIXmania. Link further attributes in the same way.

[.instruction]
Linking attributes:

. Go to *Setup » Item » Attributes*. +
→ An overview of all existing attributes opens.
. Expand the attribute *colour*.
. Click on the *Market variation* tab.
. Select *PIXmania* from the *Market* drop-down list. +
→ A second drop-down list *PIXmania* is displayed.
. Select the attribute *Colour* from the *PIXmania* drop-down list.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

==  Activating the payment method

Activate the <<payment/managing-payment-methods#, payment method>> *PIXmania Payment* as described below.

[.instruction]
Activating the PIXmania payment method:

. Go to *Setup » Orders » Payment » Methods*.
. Place a check mark next to the setting *Also show inactive*.
. Open the folder *International*.
. Click on *PIXmania Payment*. +
→ The *Settings* tab opens.
. Carry out the settings. Pay attention to the explanations given in <<settings-payment-method-pixmania>>.
. Place a check mark next to the option *Active*.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.
