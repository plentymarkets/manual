== OTTO

link:https://www.otto.de/[OTTO^]{nbsp}icon:external-link[] is part of the Otto Group and one of Germany’s most successful companies for online mail order business. +

OTTO is an online shop, which means that you sell your items under OTTO’s name. OTTO also takes care of the customer communication.

* OTTO also offers the <<markets/otto/otto-market#, marketplace OTTO Market>>. On OTTO Market, you are shown as the seller of your products. However, communication with customers is managed by OTTO. You can also sell products on OTTO Market with plentymarkets. +

IMPORTANT: For data protection reasons, the content of the OTTO page is only available for customers who have a contract with OTTO. OTTO partners can find the OTTO page in our link:https://forum.plentymarkets.com/[forum^]{nbsp}icon:external-link[] within the OTTO category.
