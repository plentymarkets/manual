== Setting up Netto eStores in plentymarkets

In order to sell items on Netto Marken-Discount, you have to set up the markets in plentymarkets. To do so, proceed as described below.

[.instruction]
Setting up Netto Marken-Discount in plentymarkets:

. Go to *Setup » Markets » Netto eStores*. +
→ The *Settings* tab opens.
. Carry out the settings. Pay attention to the explanations given in <<#settings-netto>>.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

== Setting the variation availability

You need to activate all variations that you want to sell on Netto Marken-Discount. This is done in the *Availability* tab of a variation within the *Item » Edit item » [Open item] » Tab: Variation ID* menu.

[TIP]
.Selling all variations of an item on Netto
====
If you want to sell all variations of an item on Netto, carry out the settings below for the item's main variation. All other variations of the item then inherit the settings of the main variation.
====

[.instruction]
Activating the variation availability for Netto:

. Go to *Item » Edit item » [Open item] » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Settings*.
. Open the variation that you want to sell on Netto.
. In the *Availability* area, active the option *Active*.
. Click on the *Availability* tab.
. In the *Markets* area, click in the selection field.  +
→ A list of all available markets is displayed.
. Activate the option *Netto*.
. Activate the option *Web API*.
. Click on *Add* (icon:plus-square[role="green"]). +
→ The market is added.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

== Setting the Show unit price-option

It is mandatory to specify whether items that you would like to sell on Netto have a unit price. Go to *Item » Edit item » [Open item] » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Settings* and activate the *Show unit price* option to indicate that an item needs to have a unit price.

[.instruction]
Activating the Show unit price-option:

. Go to *Item » Edit item » [Open item] » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Settings*.
. In the *Dimensions* area, activate the option *Show unit price*. +
→ The filter attribute *Grundpreispflicht* (Mandatory unit price) is set to *ja* (yes) when exporting the XML file.

If you do not activate the *Show unit price* option, then *nein* (no) is transferred and indicates that the item does not need an obligatory unit price.

icon:map-signs[] *_Or:_* As an alternative, you can create a property for mandatory unit prices. This property overwrites the setting for the *Show unit price* option. For further information, refer to the <<Creating the property Mandatory unit price, Creating the property Mandatory unit price>> chapter.

=== Creating the property energy efficiency

To comply with EU regulations on energy efficiency that came into force on 1 January 2015, you have to provide information on the energy efficiency of certain items to sell them on Netto eStores markets. In addition, you have to make product data sheets available to potential buyers of these items. Create the following three <<item/managing-items#480, properties>>: *Energy efficiency class*, *Energy label*, and *Product data sheet*.

[.instruction]
Creating the property energy efficiency class:

. Go to *Setup » Item » Characteristics*.
. Click on the *New property* tab.
. Enter the name into the *Name (internal)* field.
. Select the property type *No*.
. Select the option *Energy efficiency* from the *Netto eStores property* drop-down list. +
→ A drop-down list opens.
. Select the energy efficiency class.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

Select the property type *File* for the properties energy label and product data sheet. Select the option *Energy label* or *Product data sheet* from the drop-down list *Netto eStores property*. The following example describes how to create the property energy label. Afterwards, create the property product data sheet the same way.

[.instruction]
Creating the property energy label:

. Go to *Setup » Item » Characteristics*.
. Click on the *New property* tab.
. Enter the name into the *Name (internal)* field.
. Select the property type *File*.
. Select the option *Energy label* from the *Netto eStores property* drop-down list.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

You then link the properties in the <<item/managing-items#480, Properties>> tab of a Netto eStores item. Proceed as described below to do so.

[.instruction]
Linking energy efficiency properties to a Netto eStores item:

. Go to *Item » Edit item*.
. Open the item.
. Click on the *Characteristics* tab.
. Activate the properties *Energy efficiency class*, *Energy label* and *Product data sheet* that you have just created.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings. +
→ The properties are linked to the item and are visible in the *Characteristics* area.
. Upload the image file of the energy label to the *Energy label* property.
. Upload the PDF file of the product data sheet to the *Product data sheet* property.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

icon:map-signs[] *_Or:_* Instead of linking the energy label property, you can also upload the energy label file to the <<item/managing-items#660, Images>> tab of the item. To do so, proceed as described below.

[.instruction]
Saving the energy label in the "Images" tab:

. Go to *Item » Edit item*.
. Open the item.
. Click on the *Images* tab.
. Add the image file of the energy label either via *Select files* or via the drag-and-drop function.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.
. Go to *Setup » Markets » Netto eStores*.
. In the *Settings* tab, enter the image's position number into the *Image position for energy label* field (table 1).
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

== Automatically cancelling unpaid orders in plentymarkets after 30 days

Orders which have not been paid by the customer are automatically cancelled at Netto Marken-Discount after 30 days. Use the <<automation/procedure-manager#, procedure manager>> to set up a procedure in order to automatically cancel unpaid Netto Marken-Discount orders in your plentymarkets system after 30 days. This way, reserved stock is released again.

[.instruction]
Setting up a procedure:

. Go to *Setup » Orders » Procedures » Tab: New procedure*.
. Click on the *Settings* tab.
. Place a check mark next to the option *Active*.
. Select a time at which the procedure is to be carried out from the *Execute every day at* drop-down list. *
*_Tip:_* We recommend to use *24:00* o’clock for this procedure.
. Enter a name.
. Click on the *Filter* tab.
. Select the filters according to <<#unpaid-orders>>.
. Click on the *Procedure* tab.
. Select a status from the *Change order status* drop-down list, e.g. *[8.1] Cancelled by customer*. +
*_Tip:_* Select a status for the *Change order status* procedure that you saved within *Setup » Orders » Settings* for the *Status of cancelled orders (No reservation of stocks)* option to cancel reservations. Neither select a status that triggers an event procedure which reports cancellations to Netto eStores.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings. +
→ The procedure is created and displayed in the *Procedures* tab.

== Creating delivery notes and return labels

The following information only applies to delivery notes and return labels for Netto Marken-Discount. It does not apply to other markets.

[IMPORTANT]
.Invoices are sent by Netto
====
Netto Marken-Discount sends the invoice to the customer. You will receive the customer's delivery address from Netto but not the customer's invoice address. The delivery address supplied by Netto Marken-Discount is saved as the delivery and invoice address in plentymarkets. You send the items to this address.
====

Proceed as follows to create <<orders/order-documents#1700, PDF templates>> for delivery notes and return labels for Netto Marken-Discount. The following example describes how to create a delivery note for Netto eStores. Afterwards, create a return slip for Netto eStores in the same way. The Netto Marken-Discount logo must be included in the PDF templates. Request the logo at Netto eStores. +

Download the PDF template for link:https://cdn02.plentymarkets.com/pmsbpnokwu6a/frontend/pdf-template_netto_simple-jan2021.pdf[Netto Marken-Discount^]{nbsp}icon:external-link[] here.

[.instruction]
Creating a delivery note for Netto eStores:

. Go to *Setup » Client » [Select client] » Locations » Germany (standard) » Documents » Delivery note*.
. Click on the tab *PDF template*.
. Click on *Add template*. +
→ An editing window opens.
. Select the *Netto* option from the *Payment method* drop-down list.
. Select a language from the *Language* drop-down list.
. In the line *Template*, click on *Choose template file*.  +
→ Upload the PDF template.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.

[IMPORTANT]
.Mandatory document entries
====
The external order number and the external customer number are mandatory on documents that belong to orders with the referrer Netto. You can normally hide these entries by selecting *NO* in the *Template* tab’s *Optional elements in the stock unit table* area of the *Setup » Client » [Select client] » Locations » Germany (standard) » Documents » Delivery note* and/or *Return label* menu. +
For Netto Marken-Discount, the external order number and the external customer number are not hidden, even if you select the option *NO* here.
====

[IMPORTANT]
.Note will be hidden
====
The text that you can save in the *Note* field in the *Template* tab's *Optional elements in the stock unit table* area of the *Setup » Client » [Select client] » Locations » Germany (standard) » Documents » Delivery note* and/or *Return label* menu is hidden on documents that belong to the referrer Netto. This is required by Netto eStores.
====
