You need to activate all variations that you want to sell on {market-availability}. This is done in the *Availability* tab of a variation within the *Item » Edit item » [Open item] » Tab: Variation ID* menu.

[TIP]
.Use group function or batch processing
====
You can edit the availability of several variations at once by using either the <<item/managing-items#100, variation group function>> or <<item/managing-items#400, batch processing>>.
====

[.instruction]
Setting the variation availability for {market-availability}:

. Go to *Item » Edit item » [Open item] » Tab: Variation ID » Tab: Settings*.
. Open the variation that you want to sell on {market-availability}. +
*_Tip:_* To sell all variations of an item on {market-availability}, carry out the settings for the item’s main variation. This activates the availability of all variations of the item.
. In the *Availability* area, activate the option *Active*.
. Click on the *Availability* tab.
. In the *Markets* area, click in the selection field. +
→ A list of all available markets is displayed.
. Activate the option *{option-availability}*.
ifdef::web-api[]
. Activate the option *Web API*.
endif::web-api[]
ifdef::idealo-direkt[]
. *_Optional:_* If you want to use idealo Checkout, also activate the option *idealo Checkout*.
endif::idealo-direkt[]
. Click on *Add* (icon:plus-square[role="green"]). +
→ The market is added.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings. +
→ The variation is available for {market-availability}.
ifdef::neckermann-provision[]
. Click on the *Multi-Channel* tab.
. Select a *commission model*.
. Select a *Item type*. +
*_Tip:_* Information about the commission model and the item type is provided by Neckermann.
. *Save* (icon:save[role="green"]) the settings.
endif::neckermann-provision[]
