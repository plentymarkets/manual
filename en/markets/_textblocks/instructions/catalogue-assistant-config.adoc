[.instruction]
Creating a catalogue in the assistant:

. Open the menu {catalogue-path}.
. Click on the assistant *{catalogue-assistant-name}*.
. Follow the instructions on the screen. If you need additional information about one of the options, expand the {number-of-boxes} below this instruction (icon:chevron-down[role="darkGrey"]).
. In the step *Summary*, check your settings to makes sure they are correct. +
*_Tip:_* Click on the arrow in the step *Summary* (icon:chevron-down[role="darkGrey"]) to show the settings.
. If everything is as it should be, click on icon:check[role="green"] *Finalise*. +
→ The catalogue is created.
. Map {mapping-fields} fields in the *Data » Catalogues* menu. +
*_Note:_* {catalogue-note}
