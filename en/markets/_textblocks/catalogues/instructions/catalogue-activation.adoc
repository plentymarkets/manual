You have set up {market} and created a catalogue for exporting your item data? Then start the export of your item data.

[.instruction]
Activating the catalogue export to {market}:

. Go to *Data » Catalogues*. +
→ The catalogues are listed.
. Scroll to the {market} catalogue.
. In the *Active/Inactive* column, move the toggle button to the right (icon:toggle-on[role="green"]). +
→ The catalogue is active. +
ifndef::price-search-engine[]
→ The catalogue data is exported to {market} {interval}{if-active}.
