== Janolaw

link:http://www.janolaw.de/agb-service/#menu[Janolaw^] is a platform for German legal issues that can provide you with current legal documents for your online store. Along with providing legal services relating to *terms and conditions* as well as providing support for your *privacy policy*, Janolaw also offers further important documents for your shop, such as *cancellation rights* and a *legal disclosure*. These documents can be ordered with or without an update service. There are different service packages available from Janolaw, which you can order on the Janolaw website.

== Signing up for Janolaw

Sign up at *Janolaw* and order the service pack that meets your needs. You will receive the *access data* that you need for setting up the interface in plentymarkets.

== Setting up Janolaw in plentymarkets

In order to set up *Janolaw* in your plentymarkets system, proceed as follows.

[.instruction]
Setting up Janolaw:

. Go to *Setup » Client » Select client » Services » Janolaw*.
. Pay attention to the explanations given in <<table-janolaw-settings>> and carry out the *settings* as desired.
. *Save* the settings.

== Inserting template variables

The data provided by Janolaw will be integrated using *template variables*.

=== Online store

Insert the template variables into the corresponding *categories* or *templates* of your online store.

Use the following *template variables* to integrate the data into your *online store*:

.*Janolaw*; template variables for the *online store*
[cols="1,3"]
|====
|Template variable |Explanation

|*$GeneralTermsAndConditions*
|*Terms and conditions*

|*$CancellationRights*
|*Cancellation rights*

|*$PrivacyPolicy*
|*Privacy policy*

|*$LegalDisclosure*
|*Legal disclosure* (required by law in some countries)
|====


=== email templates

Insert the template variables into the corresponding section of your *email template*. Template variables that have the extension *Text* need to be entered into the corresponding *Text* tab. Similarly, ones that have an *HTML* extension need to be entered into the *HTML* tab.

Use the following *template variables* to integrate the data into your *email templates*:

.*Janolaw*; template variables for the *email templates*
[cols="1,3"]
|====
|Template variable |Explanation

|*$GeneralTermsConditionsText*, *$GeneralTermsConditionsHTML*
|*Terms and conditions*

|*$CancellationRightsText*, *$CancellationRightsHTML*
|*Cancellation rights*

|*$PrivacyPolicyText*, *$PrivacyPolicyHTML*
|*Privacy policy*

|*$LegalDisclosureText*, *$LegalDisclosureHTML*
|*Legal disclosure* (required by law in some countries)
|====


== Menu: Legal information

In this menu, check to make sure that all data was transmitted and displayed correctly.
