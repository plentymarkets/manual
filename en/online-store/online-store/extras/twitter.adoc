== General information about Twitter

link:http:///www.twitter.com[Twitter^] is a microblogging service which allows registered users to send text-based posts of up to 140 characters and receive such messages from other users. Tweets can be directly transmitted from plentymarkets to Twitter.

[TIP]
.Shortening the URL with bit.ly
====
The online service *bit.ly* gives you the possibility of shortening very long URLs on their website. If you saved a valid configuration for the URL shortening service, bit.ly, then the URLs that plentymarkets automatically generates for tweets will be directly shortened. For further information about configuring bit.ly, refer to the <<omni-channel/online-store/setting-up-clients/extras/bit-ly#, bit.ly>> page of the manual.
====

== Setting up Twitter in plentymarkets

In order to set up and use Twitter in plentymarkets, you need to have a Twitter account which you receive when link:http:///www.twitter.com[registering^] with Twitter.

[[image-adding-twitter-account]]
.adding a Twitter account to plentymarkets
image::omni-channel/online-store/setting-up-clients/extras/assets/EN-Settings-Client-Global-Settings-Services-Twitter-01.png[]

[.instruction]
Setting up Twitter:

. Go to *Setup » Client » Settings » Services » Twitter*.
. Click on the *green plus icon* to add a Twitter account to plentymarkets (<<image-adding-twitter-account>> , red arrow). +
→ You will be forwarded to Twitter to confirm the interface to plentymarkets. After you have confirmed the interface you will be taken back to your plentymarkets system.
. Reload the *Setup » Client » Settings » Services » Twitter* menu. +
→ The validity of your registration will be displayed (<<image-valid-twitter-account>> , blue arrow).

The connection to the plentymarkets interface will be displayed in your Twitter account under link:https://twitter.com/account/connections[https://twitter.com/account/connections^]. +
If the registration is not valid or if you went to your Twitter account and revoked the right of plentymarkets to access the Twitter web service, then plentymarkets will acknowledge this and will give you the possibility to re-register.

=== Setting up Twitter on the homepage

Link your Twitter account to the homepage.

[[image-selecting-twitter-friends-timeline]]
.selecting the *Twitter friends timeline* element
image::omni-channel/online-store/setting-up-clients/extras/assets/EN-Settings-Client-Global-Settings-Services-Twitter-03.png[]

[.instruction]
Displaying the Twitter service on your plentymarkets homepage:

. Go to *Start » Homepage*.
. Select the *Twitter friends timeline* element from the *Elements* drop-down list (<<image-selecting-twitter-friends-timeline>>).
. Click on the *green plus* above the words *New element*. +
→ The Twitter element will be added to the homepage.
. Click on the *wrench icon* in the upper right to open the element.
. Select your *Twitter account* from the drop-down list (<<image-selecting-twitter-account>>).
. *Save* the settings.

== Sending tweets from the homepage

Send tweets from the homepage and receive messages posted by people you follow.

.entering a Twitter message
image::omni-channel/online-store/setting-up-clients/extras/assets/EN-Settings-Client-Global-Settings-Services-Twitter-05.png[]

[.instruction]
Sending a tweet:

. Go to *Start » Homepage*.
. Click on the *plus icon* in the Twitter element to open your Twitter account.
. Enter a message into the text field. +
→ You can see the number of characters still remaining directly above this field.
. Click on the *save icon* to send the message.

== Sending tweets with the procedure manager

Use the procedure manager to tweet information about your live shopping offers every day. For further information about creating the procedure *Tweet live shopping offers*, refer to the <<omni-channel/online-store/extras/live-shopping#100, Procedure manager>> page of the manual.
